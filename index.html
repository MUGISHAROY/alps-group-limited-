Company Overview

Founded in 2005, Alps Group Limited is a professional construction and engineering company committed to delivering sustainable infrastructure solutions across Uganda and beyond.

We have built a strong reputation for quality, reliability, and timely project delivery through nearly two decades of industry leadership.
